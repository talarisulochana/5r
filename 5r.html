Jr.NTR

Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr., popularly known as Jr. NTR, is a well-known Indian actor and television presenter who works primarily in Telugu cinema. He was born on May 20, 1983, in Hyderabad, India. He comes from a prominent film and political family; he is the grandson of the legendary Telugu actor and former Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao (NTR).
